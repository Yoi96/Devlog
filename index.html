Game Introduction:

It is a maze game, the player can find the coins and key in this game. Press the space bar to jump.
Press "P" on the keyboard to pause the game. or exit the game.
Press "1" on the keyboard to check the map.
You can see where you are by jumping above.

30% of the damage will be taken when encountering a trap. (If your HP becomes 0 will be forced to jump to the Main Menu page)
This is a maze game to find an exit.
Victory conditions: Find the key or coins.

Level 1:

The player must to find over 5 coins or equal 5 coins.


Level 2:

The player must to find over 10 coins or equal 10 coins. Also, the player must to find a key to open the door.
